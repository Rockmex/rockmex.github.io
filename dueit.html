Due It App

Thoughts & Notes & Comments

The reason I built this app was I am a procrastinator. I rely on task management App to help me get my things organize timely so that I can finish them on time. I used several apps, but to have full features, you eventually have to pay. Moreover, I cannot find a app that has all the features I want to use. Therefore, I decided to build my own Task-Management App.
Coincidentally, my friends are planning on their graduation project, and they have a similar idea. So, we formed a team to work on it.

Due It is a productive To-Do list, memo, and task management Android App aim to help users overcome procrastination, be efficient and focused, organize tasks, and record upcoming events.

Due It has a pet system. To motivate users, rewards are given after completing each tasks. Rewards are used as the currency in the pet system.

Main Features include:

  • Pet system.
  • Pomodoro countdown.
  • Create daily routine list.
  • Create events reminder list.
  • Make weekly schedule
  • Comming Soon
