Animon

Game Jam

Animon is Slay the Spire meets Pokémon. It combines the procedural roguelike gameplay of Slay the Spire with the monster capturing and turn-based combat of Pokémon.

There are several viable strategies in the game:

  • Capturing enemy monsters
  • amassing a collection of items
  • or evolving your monsters to their most powerful stage
The game features 58 monsters, 120 abilities, 12 elemental types with unique status effects, 27 items, and 11 map locations.

Due It App

Android

Due It is a productive To-Do list, memo, and task management Android App aim to help users overcome procrastination, be efficient and focused, organize tasks, and record upcoming events.

Due It has a pet system. To motivate users, rewards are given after completing each tasks. Rewards are used as the currency in the pet system.

Main Features in Due It:

  • Pet system.
  • Pomodoro countdown.
  • Create daily routine list.
  • Create events reminder list.
  • Make weekly schedule
